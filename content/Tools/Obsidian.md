Obsidian is a note-taking app that focuses on the links that you make between topics/ideas/notes. This can be used to organize the ideas you have for your project, like its story, how that ties to your characters, how those tie into the game's theme, how that ties into the game's mechanics, and so on. It generally helps when you want to find out ways that things are related to each other, and it can help you realize certain connections between things that you haven't realized before.

I am currently using Obsidian to manage my original characters and the lore they have. Here's what the graph of all of my notes looks like so far (it's still a work in progress, and each note has a pretty serviceable amount of writing in them too.)

![[Pasted image 20250202233302.png]]

It can also be paired with [Quartz](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz) to turn Obsidian Vaults into readable webpages, and you are looking a Quartz website right now! If you are curious, there should be a graph view for this site on the right if you are on desktop.

I recommend that you use Obsidian to take down your notes and ideas for your projects, since the linking makes you think about all of the related aspects of an idea/note as you are writing it, and it's also just plain fun to see your project have a big web of ideas connected together like that.
