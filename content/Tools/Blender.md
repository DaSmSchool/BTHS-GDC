Blender is an open source 2D/3D design and modeling tools that is pretty much the ultimate visual creativity tool ever. It allows you to make 3D models that you can then texture, rig, animate, add fancy effects to, pretty much anything that you would expect anything 3D to do. Its 2D stuff is also pretty nice, though I have not played around with that very much since I am not a 2D digital artist. It's essential for pretty much any indie 3D game workflow!
