Welcome to the BTHS Game Development Club resource site!

I wanted to find some kind of way to show all of the cool resources that have contributed to my passion for game dev so far, so here it is! I have known, seen and tried pretty much every resource that I have listed here, and I highly recommend that you try a lot of these!

Since this is mostly just a directory for these resources, I also highly recommend that you check out the direct links to the resources themselves rather than just relying on the short descriptions I made for them, especially for the [[Other cool sites and channels and stuff|Other cool sites]] section. I can only say so much about each and every resource collectively, so if you are particularly interested in some specific software/software types, look up more stuff about it on the internet! The goal of this site is to act as a jumping point to discover more stuff on your own!

Other than that, have fun looking around!
