I'm almost certain you might have heard of Roblox before, but it's a game platform that lets any of its users create and share games, as well as play games made by other users. Most games made with Roblox are 3D, and have an existing online framework that its games use as their backbone. Roblox's editor, Roblox Studio, is similarly designed to other modern engines, and can also be overwhelming to those without any guidance. Roblox Studio also uses Lua as its primary programming language.

If you really want to make a larger scale game and make it more accessible online, try this one out!
I would not recommend making your first, smaller projects with Roblox Studio, since learning both the editor and programming at the same time would likely be too much for many people starting out.

Roblox Studio is available on Windows and Mac.
