Unity Engine is pretty much the most popular 2D/3D engine used by... everyone, though people are leaving unity due to its recent controversies. You can look those up if you want to, but in short, lots of people lost trust in the company that owns unity because of their pricing plans and decisions. There are tons of tutorials for how to use Unity, though I haven't had enough experience with both Unity or its tutorials to know if it's any good or not.

If you want to consider Unity, try and make some smaller projects in other engines to transition into Unity as smoothly as possible. Learning the layout of the engine, object oriented programming and how to program at all at the same time will very likely be pretty overwhelming.

Unity's editor is available on Windows, Mac and Linux.
