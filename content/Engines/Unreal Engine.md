Unreal Engine is a very powerful 3D engine, and it is geared to developers who would utilize its graphics intensive features. While it is indeed very robust, I would not use this engine as someone who wants to start making games, and it's really only useful for those who *really really* know what they are doing when making games.

Unreal Engine is available on Windows/Mac/Linux.
